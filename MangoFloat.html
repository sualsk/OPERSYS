Mango Float Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • 1 pack graham crackers
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and heavy cream. Mix until well combined.
  2. Arrange a layer of graham crackers at the bottom of a rectangular dish.
  3. Pour a portion of the milk mixture over the layer of graham crackers, spreading it evenly.
  4. Add a layer of sliced mangoes on top of the milk mixture.
  5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sliced mangoes on top.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.
  7. Slice and serve cold. Enjoy your delicious mango float!
